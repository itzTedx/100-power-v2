    ## Product Overview
    Power-10 is a high-performance maintenance lubricant spray, engineered with nano-penetrating technology and powered by Power Booster. It penetrates deep to eliminate rust, corrosion, and friction at the micro level—making it up to 20 times stronger than traditional sprays. 
    
    Ideal for both industrial and household use, Power-10 dramatically extends the life and reliability of metal parts, tools, and electrical components—even in extreme conditions.

    - Fast & Deep Penetration into metal surfaces
    - Breaks down rust and corrosion efficiently
    - Provides anti-friction and anti-wear protection
    - Removes carbon deposits for optimal engine performance
    - Prevents micro-metallurgical welding and metal expansion
    - Extends tool and equipment life by up to 4-5x
    - Withstands extreme temperature, dust, and moisture
    - Increases machining efficiency with up to 200% longer cutting tool life
    - Protects electrical parts from moisture and corrosion buildup
    - Keeps mechanisms clean and free from deposits

    <DirectionsTabs>
        1. ### Check Compatibility
    
            Refer to your vehicle's manual to ensure Power 85W90 API SP meets the recommended specifications.
   
        2. ### Drain Old Oil
    
            Completely remove the old engine oil to prevent contamination and maintain optimal performance.
   
        3. ### Replace Oil Filter
    
            Always install a new oil filter before adding fresh oil for best results.
   
        4. ### Add Power Oil
    
            Pour the recommended quantity of Power 85W90 into the engine through the filler cap.
   
        5. ### Check Oil Level
    
            Use the dipstick to verify oil level and adjust as necessary before starting the engine.
   
        6. ### Run & Inspect
    
            Start the engine and let it idle briefly to circulate the oil, then check for leaks.
   
        7. ### Monitor Regularly
    
            Check oil levels periodically and top up if needed. Follow scheduled oil change intervals for extended engine life.

    <SafetyTabs>
        <TabContent value="handling">
            - Use mechanical ventilation in areas where vapors may accumulate.
            - Ensure local exhaust ventilation at the source of vapors.
            - Provide local ventilation at bulk vessel openings when handling heated oil.
            - Store at temperatures not exceeding 60–70°C.
            - Avoid extremely high temperatures and direct sunlight.
            - Do not store near oxidizing agents or flammable materials.
            - Prevent overheating during storage or handling.
        </TabContent>
        <TabContent value="first-aid">
            - **Eyes**: In case of eye contact, rinse immediately with plenty of water and seek medical advice.
            - **Skin**: If skin contact occurs, wash thoroughly with soap and water.
            - **Ingestion**: Do not induce vomiting. Give 500 ml of water to drink and seek medical attention immediately.
            - **Inhalation**: Move the affected person to fresh air and keep them at rest.
        </TabContent>
        <TabContent value="disposal">
            - Dispose of used oil and empty containers responsibly and in accordance with local or national environmental regulations.
