  ## Product Overview
  100 Power Airolube 6086 F is a highly refined, medium-viscosity mineral oil containing an extreme pressure additive as well as additives to provide good oxidation and corrosion protection. It is suitable as an extreme pressure lubricant for heavily loaded pins, bushes, and gear mechanisms.

  ## About Gear Fluids
  100 Power gear fluids for aviation applications ensure the lubrication, robustness, and reliability of equipment. Their anti-wear, resistance to oxidation, corrosion protection, and high viscosity index properties make all our products exceed in many cases the requirements set forth in US and European standards.
