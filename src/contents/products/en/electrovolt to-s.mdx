  ## Product Overview
  100 Power ElectroVolt TO-S is a premium synthetic insulating fluid engineered for railway transformers and high-voltage equipment. With superior oxidation stability, very low pour point, and a high fire point, it ensures long service life, safety, and dependable performance in extreme conditions.

  ## About Transformer Oil
  100 Power Transformer Oil is a high-performance insulating and cooling oil designed for electrical transformers and related equipment. Formulated to provide excellent dielectric strength, thermal stability, and oxidation resistance, it ensures reliable operation under heavy electrical loads and extreme temperatures. By protecting against corrosion, sludge formation, and moisture, this oil extends transformer life, reduces maintenance, and maintains optimal performance in power distribution and industrial applications.

  ## Meet & Exceed
  - IEC 61099 (Synthetic/ester transformer fluids)
  - ASTM D6871 (Synthetic ester based oils)
  - Compatible with modern ecofriendly transformer requirements
