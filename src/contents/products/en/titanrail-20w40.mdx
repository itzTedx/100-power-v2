    ## Product Overview
  100 Power TitanRail 20W-40 is formulated for 4-stroke locomotive

## About 100 Power TitanRail
The 100 Power TitanRail 4-Stroke series is formulated for modern locomotive diesel engines,
providing options across low, medium, and high ash with tailored TBN ranges to suit diverse OEM
requirements and fuel conditions. These oils offer excellent oxidation and thermal stability,
advanced sludge and varnish control, and strong wear resistance, ensuring extended oil life and
maximum protection. TitanRail 4-Stroke is trusted in heavy-haul freight, high-speed passenger,
and yard locomotives, maintaining engine cleanliness, efficiency, and reliability to minimize
downtime and reduce operating costs.
